Who We Are

About AMCE

AMCE Management is an artist management company dedicated to discovering, developing, and managing the next generation of music talent.

We handle everything from branding and digital strategy to distribution and marketing — so our artists can focus on what they do best: making music.

Our roster includes emerging artists making waves on platforms like Spotify, YouTube, TikTok, and more. We believe in long-term artist development, creative freedom, and building authentic connections with audiences worldwide.
